Yun Zhang

Biography

Yun Zhang is a Ph.D. student in Data Science and Analytics (DSA) at the Guangzhou Campus of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), supervised by Professor Bingyi Jing. He received his Master’s degree from the University of Hong Kong (HKU) in 2021, and his Bachelor’s degree from Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech) in 2020. His research interests include but not limited to machine learning, computational statistics and data visualization.
